Chocolate-Covered Mini Olive Oil Cakes

Video

 

Recipes

INGREDIENTS
  1. 2 cups of flour
  2. 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  3. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  4. 1 1/2 Tbsp. of finely chopped rosemary
  5. 1 cup of sugar
  6. 3 large eggs
  7. 1 cup of olive oil
  8. 1/2 cup fresh orange juicerosemary
  9. 1 1/2 Tbsp. of orange zest
  10. 2 Tbsp. of whole milk
  11. Melted chocolate
  12. Sugar orange twist for garnish
  13. Rosemary leaves for garnish
DIRECTIONS
  1. Combine dry ingredients in a medium bowl, then set aside. Mix together wet ingredients.
  2. Slowly add the dry ingredients to wet ingredients until you have a consistent batter.
  3. Oil your mini muffin tin. Spoon enough batter so it’s 1/2 way to the top. Bake at 350°F/175°C for eight to 10 minutes. Remove from oven and let it cool to room temp.
  4. Dip each mini olive oil cake in melted chocolate, top with a sugar orange twist and a few rosemary leaves.
  5. Let the chocolate set, then enjoy!
