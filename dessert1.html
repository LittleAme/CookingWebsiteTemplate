BBQ Chicken Pinwheels

Video

 

Recipes

INGREDIENTS
  1. 1 refrigerated pizza crust
  2. 1/2 cup BBQ sauce
  3. 1 1/2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  4. 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  5. 1 cup shredded cheese
  6. 1/4 cup parsley, chopped
DIRECTIONS
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F/220°C. Roll out the pizza crust into a rectangle.
  2. Spread on the BBQ sauce. Top with chicken, onion, cheese, and parsley. Roll it up longways, and cut into slices.
  3. Bake face-up for 15 minutes. Enjoy!
