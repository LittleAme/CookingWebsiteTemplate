Fried Banana Ice Cream

Video

 

Recipes

INGREDIENTS
  1. 2 ripe bananas
  2. 2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour
  3. 2 tsp rice flour
  4. 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. 1/2 tsp salt
  6. 1 tsp sugar
  7. Oil for deep frying
  8. 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream
  9. 1 Tbsp. chocolate syrup
  10. 1 Tbsp. sweetened condensed milk
DIRECTIONS
  1. Mash the bananas in a bowl. Stir in all-purpose flour, rice flour, baking soda and salt until mixed. Mix in sugar.
  2. Heat up cooking oil for deep frying in a small pan. Once the oil is hot, scoop up a spoonful, roughly a tablespoon, of batter into the frying pan. Deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Place the fritters on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
  4. Serve the fritters with a scoop of vanilla ice cream. Drizzle with chocolate syrup and sweetened condensed milk. Enjoy!
