Strawberries and Cream Cupcakes

Video

 

Recipes

INGREDIENTS
Yields: 12 cupcakes
  1. 1 lb fresh strawberries
  2. 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  3. 3 Tbsp. powdered sugar
  4. 1 box yellow cake mix plus ingredients to prepare it
DIRECTIONS
  1. While they bake, finely chop five to seven strawberries until they are almost a pulp. Cut the tops off of 12 strawberries. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, beat the whipping cream, chopped strawberries, and powdered sugar until the mixture forms stiff peaks when the beaters are lifted up. Set aside.
  3. Let the cupcakes cool completely, then cut a hole in each one about the width of a quarter (leave room at the bottom of the cupcake).
  4. Press a strawberry, pointed side down into each cupcake, then top with whipped cream. Enjoy!
