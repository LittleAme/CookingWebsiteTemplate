★★ CHOCOLATE MILLE CREPE ★★

★ VIDEO ★

 

★ RECIPES ★

Ingredients
  1. 1 extra-large egg
  2. 1 cup milk
  3. 4 tbsp sugar
  4. 3 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  5. 1/2 cup+1 tbsp flour
  6. 15 strawberries
  7. mint
  8. chocolate syrup
  9. 1/2 cup heavy cream + 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
DIRECTIONS
  1. Combine the milk and sugar and microwave until warm.
  2. Whisk the egg in a bowl and mix in the milk & sugar mixture in thirds.
  3. Sift in the cocoa powder and flour and mix.
  4. Let the batter sit in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  5. In the meantime, whip your cream and slice the strawberries.
  6. Pour a thin layer of batter onto a heated frying pan and cook until both sides are dry.
  7. Repeat until all the batter is cooked.
  8. Put a sheet of crepe onto a plate, followed by whipped cream and then the sliced strawberries. Repeat until you've used up all the crepes and strawberries.
  9. Top with more strawberries, a sprig of mint and chocolate syrup.
  10. The dessert is ready to serve.
