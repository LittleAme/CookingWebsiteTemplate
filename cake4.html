Cookies Dough Cheesecake

Video

 

Recipes

INGREDIENTS
  1. 3 8-oz packages of cream cheese, softened
  2. 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. 1 cup sour cream
  4. 12 tsp vanilla extract
  5. 3 eggs
  6. 1 package pre-made cookie dough optional extra for topping
DIRECTIONS
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F180°C. Press a package of pre-made cookie dough into a springform pan. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese and sugar until fluffy.
  2. Add sour cream and vanilla and mix. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing thoroughly after each one.
  3. Pour into springform pan. Optional drop half teaspoonfuls of cookie dough on top of the cheesecake batter.
  4. You can remove any link to our website from this website template.
  5. Bake for one hour, cool completely, then refrigerate overnight.
